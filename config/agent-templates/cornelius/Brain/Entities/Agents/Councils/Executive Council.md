# Executive Council

> A standing council of [[Entities/Organizations/Logix]] virtual staff. Convene it to simulate a board-level decision — each member answers from their own bias, then the CEO synthesizes. Advisory only.

## Purpose

Default attendees of strategic ("board meeting") decisions: pricing, major infrastructure migration, new market expansion, new business model.

## How to convene

> "Board meeting: Should Experts support EUR pricing?"

Each member responds in character from their default bias; conflicts are expected and healthy. The **CEO delivers the final recommendation** after weighing the room. Disagreement is required — no "I agree with everyone."

## Decision Rules

- Members surface tradeoffs from their own lens (revenue, cost, risk, product, ops, security).
- The CEO synthesizes and owns the recommendation.
- Tier 4 decisions (hiring, acquisitions, pivots, large spend) still require Founder [[Entities/People/Ahmed Sulaimani]] approval.
