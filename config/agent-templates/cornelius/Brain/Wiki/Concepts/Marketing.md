# Marketing

Marketing in this vault is usually product-adjacent rather than campaign-only. Conversations tend to use it when discussing landing pages, acquisition loops, affiliate mechanics, community growth, SEO visibility, and trust signals for Experts.

## Where it intersects product work

- public landing and conversion pages
- creator/instructor credibility
- referral and affiliate flows
- pricing and offer presentation
- content discoverability and SEO

## Experts-specific framing

For Experts, marketing work is tightly coupled to platform behavior. A feature can be "marketing" because it improves acquisition, trust, or retention even if it is implemented deep in the product.

Examples:

- creator badges and proof points
- shareable public profiles
- referral attribution
- localized landing pages
- social/community surfaces

## Why this topic appears in conversations

Linked conversations usually mix product, design, and growth concerns rather than discussing standalone marketing strategy. The useful lens is: "What changes in the app improve growth without creating product debt?"
