# Instructor Certification

Instructor certification covers the workflow for approving experts to teach, defining trust signals on their profile, and gating what they are allowed to publish on the platform.

## Context in Experts

Within Experts, certification is tied to instructor onboarding, profile credibility, and access to creator workflows. It belongs with subscriptions, payouts, and publishing permissions, not just profile cosmetics.

## Typical concerns

- What evidence an instructor must provide before approval
- Whether certification is manual, automated, or staged
- Which badges, levels, or labels appear publicly
- What publishing rights certification unlocks

## Product role

Certification is one of the mechanisms that lets Experts present itself as an expert-led learning platform rather than an open marketplace with no quality bar. It also affects learner trust, pricing justification, and legal risk.

## Likely integration points

- Instructor onboarding and profile review
- Course and event publishing permissions
- Badge or level display in creator and learner-facing surfaces
- Admin moderation and verification decisions
