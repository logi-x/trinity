# Community

Community covers the social layer of Experts: posts, discussions, groups, comments, reactions, and the engagement loops around courses and events.

## Context in Experts

Experts treats community as one of the platform's core domains. The November 2025 decision log also records it as one of the four confirmed platform functions, even though soft launch scope trims some community pieces until after launch.

## Product role

- Keeps learners engaged between purchases
- Gives instructors a way to build audience and trust
- Creates a place for discussions, updates, and peer interaction
- Supports long-term retention beyond one-off enrollments

## Implementation shape

Community appears across both web and native contexts:

- Experts lists community and discussions as first-class product areas
- Experts OS includes Community under `Experts/Features/`
- [[Wiki/Concepts/WebSockets]] is relevant because notifications, reactions, and presence depend on realtime behavior

## Launch posture

The current business posture is pragmatic: community is strategically important, but some social features are lower priority than courses, events, and subscription plans for the first launch window.
