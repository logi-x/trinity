# Embeddings

Dense **vector representations** of text (courses, instructors, content) used
for semantic search and similarity — the retrieval substrate behind
[[Wiki/Concepts/Recommendations]] and AI-powered discovery.

## In the Experts app

- Stored and queried in **PostgreSQL via `pgvector`** — see [[Wiki/Concepts/Postgres]].
- Generated by an embedding pipeline over long-lived content; indexed for
  approximate nearest-neighbour search.
- Part of the broader AI surface — see [[Wiki/Concepts/AI Features]].
