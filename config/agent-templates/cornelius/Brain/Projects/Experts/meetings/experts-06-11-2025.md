## 3-Bullet Summary

- Logix demoed the Experts platform in-progress — courses module (creation flow, lessons, quizzes, ratings, draft/publish), events module, and instructor dashboard. Developer estimates ~65% complete; client perceived ~30–40% based on what was visible, with dashboard, community, and linking still outstanding.
- Team aligned on a phased soft launch: deliver Courses + Events + Subscription/Plan billing first (revised target: mid-July 2026), then add Community, Discussions, and Posts post-launch. A dedicated server will be provisioned at soft-launch readiness.
- Key blockers on client side: logo/brand assets for landing page, category/subcategory list, 1 sample course for testing, and the instructor ToS draft (8–9 clauses already written by Adnan from prior contracts).
