## Positioning

Experts is an online learning platform and course marketplace built for the Arab region. It helps experts create, publish, and sell courses, while giving learners a modern way to discover trusted education, enroll in programs, watch lessons, join live sessions, and grow through a bilingual digital learning experience.

Unlike generic global platforms, Experts is designed around regional realities: Arabic and English support, full RTL and LTR interfaces, local commerce flows, instructor tools, learner dashboards, social features, and Saudi-compliant invoicing.

## Core Narrative

Use this sentence as the backbone of the profile:

**Experts is the platform where expertise becomes structured learning, trusted digital products, and scalable educational businesses.**

Supporting ideas:

- Built for both learners and experts
- Designed for Arabic-first and bilingual experiences
- Combines content, commerce, community, and operations in one platform
- Enables education businesses to scale without stitching together multiple tools

## Visual Direction For InDesign

Base the redesign on the current slide system, not the old company profile styling.

### Color

- Primary accent: `oklch(0.58 0.17 255.97)`
- Light background: `oklch(0.98 0 0)` / white surfaces
- Dark background: `oklch(0.08 0 0)`
- Muted text: `oklch(0.72 0.01 270)`
- Destructive red if needed for contrast accents only: `oklch(0.7 0.19 22.23)`

### Typography

- English display: DM Sans Bold
- English body: Inter
- Arabic display: Readex Pro
- Arabic body: Almarai

### Shape Language

- Card radius: 20px
- Media radius: 24px
- Small fields/chips: 12px
- Use soft surface shadows, not hard drop shadows

### Layout Language

- Alternate between light and dark spreads
- Use large editorial headlines
- Use subtle grid backgrounds on selected light pages
- Use radial glow gradients on hero and section-intro pages
- Prefer modular cards, split layouts, stat blocks, and clean diagrams

### Image Direction

Use a mix of:

- product UI screenshots
- instructor/learner lifestyle imagery
- close crops of dashboard/product surfaces
- abstract gradient or geometry backgrounds where photography is not needed

Avoid stock-heavy pages with too much generic business imagery.
