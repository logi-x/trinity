## Asset approach

For each spread, use one of these:

- real product screenshot from Experts
- current design-system OG image as temporary stand-in
- AI-generated editorial image where a product shot is not the strongest visual

Keep all visuals premium and restrained. Avoid generic stock business teams.

12. events page

## Screenshot quality rules

- Use desktop ratios unless the spread specifically benefits from mobile
- Hide browser chrome if possible
- Prefer clean, real data over lorem ipsum where available
- Avoid screens with too many tiny unreadable labels
- Capture both Arabic and English from similar states when possible
- Keep shadows and framing consistent when placed into InDesign
