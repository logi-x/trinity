## Current position

Active work is **Phase 7** (1 of 3 plans executed). This phase migrates existing cert data to the deep schema and wires up the NEEDS_INFO flow, application history, and admin review decisions.

Blocking chain to complete v1.1: **7 → 8 → 9**

Phases 2 and 3 (completion certificates + learning polish) are fully untouched.

---

## What's still needed for mid-July launch

Three large feature areas flagged in [[Action-Tracker]] are in progress with no completed phases yet:

1. **Courses module** — lessons, quizzes, assessments, quiz time limits, draft/publish
2. **Subscription/plan billing** — required before any course publish action; Noon infrastructure is hardened, access gating not yet built
3. **Events module** — location, capacity, pricing, categories, agenda, refund policy

Phase 14 (admin: users management, revenue visibility, analytics) is planned but not started.
