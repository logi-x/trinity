# Experts Dashboard

↑ [[Entities/Projects/Experts|Experts]]

Cross-folder views over everything tagged `#experts` — the one thing Bases do that the file tree,
graph, and tag pane can't: collapse folders into status/date-driven triage. See also the
[[Projects/Experts/Experts App/project|Experts App project]] and its
[[Projects/Experts/Experts App/Experts App Map.canvas|map]].

One Base, switch tabs:

- **⚠ Overdue actions** · **🔴 Open high/critical bugs** · **🗳 Decisions due for review** — needs-attention triage pulled from `Actions/`, `Raw/bugs/`, and `Decisions/` regardless of folder (filters are case-robust; `status`/`severity` casing is inconsistent across notes).
- **🐞 All open bugs by severity** — full open-bug list.
- **📚 All Experts notes (by category)** · **🕑 Recently touched** — every `#experts` note in the vault, the single entry point across bugs, plans, sources, actions, decisions, and docs.
