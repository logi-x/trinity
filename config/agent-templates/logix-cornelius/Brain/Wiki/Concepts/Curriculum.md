# Curriculum

The **structure of a course** in the Experts LMS: how learning content is
organised and sequenced. A course is composed of **modules**, each module of
**lessons**, and assessments (**quizzes** / **exams**) are attached at the
module or course level.

## In the Experts app

- Curriculum is authored in the creator **curriculum editor** (modules,
  lessons, inline quiz/exam editors) under the course catalog domain.
- Structural changes to a **published** course are governed by the unified
  **7-day significant-change cooldown** (not a permanent lock) — see
  [[Wiki/Concepts/Assessments]] for the quiz-scoring integrity guard that
  sits alongside it.
- Certificates and completion are earned by progressing through the
  curriculum — see [[Wiki/Concepts/Instructor Certification]].
