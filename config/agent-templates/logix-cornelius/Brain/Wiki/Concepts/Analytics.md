# Analytics

Analytics in this vault usually means operational product insight for Experts: understanding what users do, where flows break, and which product surfaces actually drive value.

## Typical use cases

- tracking conversion through signup, payment, and publishing flows
- measuring engagement in courses, events, and community features
- exposing admin/reporting views for business decisions
- debugging where a payment or user journey failed

## Product framing

Good analytics here is less about vanity dashboards and more about decision support:

- where users drop off
- which creators or offers perform
- whether acquisition loops work
- where operational failures hide

## Repeated conversation concerns

- instrumenting business-critical events instead of everything
- tying analytics to identifiable workflow steps
- keeping payment and billing failures observable
- distinguishing product insight from noisy logging
