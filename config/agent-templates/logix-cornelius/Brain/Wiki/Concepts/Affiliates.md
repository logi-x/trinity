## What it usually means

In the vault, affiliate discussions are rarely about generic referrals. They usually mean platform-specific business logic such as:

- referral attribution
- coupon or link ownership
- commission eligibility
- payout timing
- admin visibility into affiliate performance

Affiliate work sits at the boundary of marketing, payments, and trust. It affects:

- partner acquisition
- revenue sharing
- reporting
- admin operations
- fraud and abuse controls
