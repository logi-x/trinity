## Overview

House of Wisdom Academy (HoWA) is a Logix-owned online education / e-learning platform. Its core is courses, instructors, students, enrollment orders, invoices and coupons, services, support, and newsletters, plus operational tooling, billing/refunds, reporting, and deployment workflows. Built on Laravel v13 + Inertia across admin, client, and server apps. An ETF/investment module also exists but is a secondary feature slated for deprecation — not part of the core. In this vault, use this note as the project hub before drilling into HoWA docs, rules, or adjacent Logix/Experts context.
