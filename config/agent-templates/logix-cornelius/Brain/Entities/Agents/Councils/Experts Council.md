# Experts Council

> A standing council of [[Entities/Organizations/Logix]] virtual staff for product decisions affecting the Experts platform. Advisory only.

## Purpose

Product decisions that affect [[Entities/Projects/Experts]] and [[Entities/Projects/Experts App]] — course experience, community, events, growth, and learning quality.

## How to convene

> "Experts product review: should we add live cohort-based courses?"

Education protects learning quality, Community and Events weigh engagement and logistics, the CPO represents the customer, and the CMO weighs growth. Tension between **quality (Education)** and **growth (CMO)** is the point.

## Decision Rules

- Education Director holds a quality veto on anything that degrades learning outcomes.
- CPO synthesizes the product recommendation; pricing/strategy escalates to the Executive Council.
