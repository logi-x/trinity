### Company profile description

Experts Company Ltd. owns and operates Experts, a bilingual online learning platform built for expert-led education in Saudi Arabia and the wider region. Experts combines a learning management system, an open course marketplace, expert creator tools, live events, payment workflows, invoicing, and social learning features into one integrated product.

The platform serves learners who want trusted practical knowledge, experts who want to turn their experience into scalable digital products, and organizations or partners that need a regional learning-commerce infrastructure. Learners can discover courses, enroll, watch lessons, track progress, and engage with relevant learning communities. Experts can publish structured learning content, manage enrollments, monetize their expertise, and understand learner activity through operational dashboards.

Experts is designed for Arabic-first markets while remaining usable internationally. The product supports Arabic and English experiences, right-to-left and left-to-right layouts, localized user experience patterns, Saudi-focused payment expectations, and invoicing workflows aligned with local compliance needs. Its commercial layer supports paid enrollment, course sales, promotions, invoices, and regional payment providers such as Stripe, Tabby, and Noon Payments.

Beyond standard course hosting, Experts is positioned as an operating system for expert-led education. The platform direction includes AI-assisted learning experiences, affiliate marketing workflows, live events and webinars, expert verification and certification, analytics, billing, and institutional partnership opportunities. This gives the company multiple growth paths across marketplace sales, expert subscriptions, events, partner programs, and business learning solutions.

## Design system

Experts: an online learning platform and course marketplace where experts create and sell
courses. Features learner dashboards, course enrollment, video players, instructor tools,
commerce (Stripe, Tabby, Noon), invoicing (ZATCA-compliant), and social features.
Supports full RTL/Arabic and LTR/English with dark and light modes.

Design system built on Tailwind CSS v4 + HeroUI v3 + Radix UI primitives.

Colors (OKLCH):

- Primary: oklch(0.58 0.17 255.97) — #2879dc (vibrant purple-blue)
- Background light: oklch(0.98 0 0) — #fafafa (light) | dark: oklch(0.08 0 0) — #09090b (dark)
- Destructive: oklch(0.7 0.19 22.23) — #e53e3e (red)
- Brand logo: green leaf (#B1DF10→#5F8A02) + blue X (#2AAECF→#2A5E9B) + text (#939393)

Typography:

- LTR display: DM Sans (100–900) | body: Inter
- RTL display: Readex Pro | body: Almarai

Border radius base: 8px — cards 20px, fields 12px, media 24px

Full RTL/Arabic support via [dir="rtl"] selectors.
Theme stored in cookie, defaults to light. Dark mode via [data-theme="dark"].
Component state via data attributes: data-invalid, data-focused, data-hovered, data-disabled.
