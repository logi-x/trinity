**Cornelius v05.26 — Thinks While You Sleep**

Three new autonomous capabilities for the knowledge base agent:

• **Incubation loop** — Rotating analytical moves (ACH, Bayesian updates, steelmanning, cross-domain bridging) applied to your active open questions on a cron schedule. Runs overnight while you are away.
• **Domain watch** — Perception layer that monitors your KB for new notes matching declared intellectual interests; auto-activates high-resonance findings into the thinking queue.
• **Insight interview** — Socratic dialogue grounded in your existing notes. Searches what you already think before asking a single question.
• **YouTube transcript extraction** — Closes the last gap in the capture layer.

The neuroscience behind the design: the hypnagogic state produces an 83% insight solution rate vs 30% for full wakefulness. The incubation loop runs at the edges — between your sessions, without your presence.
