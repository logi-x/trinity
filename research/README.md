# Research

This is where **Ability AI** publishes its open research work.

Papers, whitepapers, and technical reports that come out of building and operating
Trinity and the agents that run on it are collected here so that anyone can read
them, cite them, and check the claims. Each paper lives in its own subfolder with a
dated PDF of the final manuscript.

## Conventions

- One subfolder per paper, named after the paper's slug.
- The final manuscript ships as a PDF named `<slug>-<YYYY-MM-DD>.pdf`. The date is
  the manuscript date, so revisions are added as new dated files rather than
  overwriting the record.
- Add a row to the table above when a paper lands.
